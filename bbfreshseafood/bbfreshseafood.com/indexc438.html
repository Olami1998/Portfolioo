FAQs About Seafood

Is frozen seafood as good as fresh?

Our flash-frozen-at-sea process actually preserves peak freshness better than "fresh" seafood that spends days in transit. By freezing within hours of harvest at -30°F, we lock in nutrients, texture, and flavor without preservatives.

How should I thaw frozen seafood?

For best results:

  1. Refrigerator method: Place sealed package in fridge for 12-24 hours

  2. Cold water method: Submerge sealed package in cold water (change water every 30 mins)
    Never thaw at room temperature or in hot water
